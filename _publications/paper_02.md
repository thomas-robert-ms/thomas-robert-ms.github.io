excerpt: 'This paper reports the synthesis and characterization of three new quinoline-substituted bicyclooctadiene photoswitches for molecular solar-thermal energy storage. The study shows that the position of the quinoline nitrogen strongly affects their absorption, energy-storage properties, thermal half-lives, and response to protonation, leading to different outcomes such as photoswitching, fluorescence, or degradation. Overall, the findings provide useful structure–property design rules for improving BOD-based photoswitches for MOST applications.'
