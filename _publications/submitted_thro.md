excerpt: 'Here, we use sequence-defined peptoids bearing an azobenzene chromophore to probe gas-phase Z→E isomerization kinetics by collision-induced isomerization on TWIMS-QToF instruments. By monitoring Z-to-E conversion as a function of Trap Cell activation voltage, we extract kinetic constants and derive effective ion temperatures using a modified Eyring framework. The resulting energy-conversion efficiency values are comparable to those reported for protein ions, while system-dependent pre-activation temperatures highlight the need for broader switchable-molecule libraries to develop reliable temperature calibration strategies.'
