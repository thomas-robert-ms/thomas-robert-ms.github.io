excerpt: 'Here, we examine the structural and gas phase stability modifications in SUMO1 induced by diethyl fumarate succination with cyclic ion mobility-mass spectrometry. Collision-induced unfolding (CIU) and sliced-CIU highlight a modified unfolding process resulting from the creation of specific charge-dipole interactions involving the appended dicarbonyl moiety. The experimental results are further supported by molecular dynamics simulations to understand at the atomistic level the mechanisms underlying the CIU of gaseous (derivatized) SUMO1 ions.'
